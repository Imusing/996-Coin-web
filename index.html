Welcome to the 996-Coin Website! This is the official home of 996-Coin (Nine Nine Six coin), a cryptocurrency designed to tip other people. 996-Coin runs on PoS 3.0, features Multi-PoS reward sharing, and uses an anti-whale staking limit of 125,000 coins per UTXO to make staking fairer and more decentralized.
